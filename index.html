Who We Are

Neurorder is a South African research and technology company focused on digital public infrastructure, alternative credit scoring, financial inclusion, and community development.

We build practical systems that connect underserved communities to opportunity through research, innovation, and technology.

Through Neurorder Research, the Alternative Credit Scoring Project, and the iNtsikelelo Foundation, we aim to create a smarter and more inclusive future.
