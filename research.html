From Informality to Inclusion

This research explores how Digital Public Infrastructure can help South Africa's informal economy become more visible, trusted and financially included.

The research combines Digital Identity, Digital Payments, Artificial Intelligence and Alternative Credit Scoring to support street vendors, youth entrepreneurs, domestic workers and micro-businesses.

Instead of depending only on traditional banking history, Neurorder investigates how transaction behaviour can become a trusted indicator of financial participation.

Our long-term vision is to contribute towards South Africa's 2030 Agenda by expanding financial inclusion through responsible technology and research.
