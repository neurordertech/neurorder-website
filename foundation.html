iNtsikelelo Foundation

A Neurorder social impact initiative supporting education, dignity, youth empowerment, and community development in South Africa.

Our Focus Support Us

Purpose of the Foundation

iNtsikelelo Foundation exists to support communities through practical giving, education support, winter relief, and youth-focused empowerment.

While Neurorder Research focuses on digital public infrastructure and future economic systems, iNtsikelelo Foundation focuses on direct human impact.

What We Focus On

Winter Support

Blankets, warm clothing, socks, and essential winter care items for vulnerable communities.

Education Support

Supporting learners through school-focused donations, motivation, and academic upliftment.

Hygiene & Dignity

Basic hygiene products and care items that protect dignity and wellbeing.

Youth Empowerment

Encouraging young people to see education, technology, and service as pathways to change.

How Support Moves

01

02

Donations, items, and resources are collected from supporters.

03

04

Deliver Impact
